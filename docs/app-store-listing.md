# Shopify App Store Listing — DropToDrop

## App Name
DropToDrop — Dropshipping Network

## Tagline
Connect with suppliers. Import products. Sell instantly. Auto-fulfill orders.

## Description (Short)
DropToDrop is the dropshipping network built for Shopify. Find verified suppliers, import products in one click, and let orders route and fulfill automatically.

## Description (Long)
DropToDrop connects Shopify merchants who want to sell (resellers) with merchants who supply products (suppliers) — creating a seamless dropshipping network within the Shopify ecosystem.

**For Resellers:**
- Browse a curated marketplace of supplier products
- Import products to your store with one click — images, descriptions, and pricing included
- Set your own markup (percentage or fixed) with AI-powered price suggestions
- Orders automatically route to suppliers when customers buy
- Tracking numbers sync back to your customers automatically
- Message suppliers directly, request samples before committing

**For Suppliers:**
- List your Shopify products on the DropToDrop marketplace
- Set wholesale pricing and control what percentage of stock is available
- Receive orders automatically from resellers
- Manage fulfillment and tracking from one dashboard
- Broadcast announcements to all your resellers
- Build your reputation with reviews and trust scores

**Key Features:**
- Smart Pricing AI — suggests optimal retail prices
- Bulk Import — import 50+ products at once
- 14 Product Categories — organized browsing
- In-App Messaging — direct supplier-reseller communication
- Dispute Resolution — report and resolve issues
- Analytics Dashboard — revenue, orders, performance metrics
- Shipping Rules — per-country rates and delivery estimates
- Sample Orders — try products before importing
- Exclusive Deals — time-limited supplier discounts
- Real-Time Sync — hourly inventory updates

**Billing:**
- Free plan: 5 products, 10 orders/month
- Standard: €29/month — unlimited products & orders, 2% fee
- Premium: €79/month — unlimited everything, 0% fees
